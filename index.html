ABOUT

Hi! I am an Incoming Ph.D. Student in CSE at the University of California, San Diego, advised by Prof. Aaron Schulman. I'm also honored to work closely with Prof. Dinesh Bharadia, Prof. Nadia Heninger, and Prof. Dave Levin . My research interests lie at the intersection of computer systems, wireless & networking, satellite network, and machine learning.
